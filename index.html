Diving Around Batanta Island in Raja Ampat

Across the Dampier Strait from Waigeo is the smallest island of Raja Ampat’s ‘Four Kings’ Batanta Island. Countless bays, beautiful beaches, pristine mangroves, and towering waterfalls are features of this remote island that few tourists make the trek to visit.

How Long Do I Need to Experience Raja Ampat?

Raja Ampat is a remote region of the Indonesian archipelago on the west side of West Papua. Because of its location and the steps it takes to get to Papua Paradise Eco Resort, it’s best to do a little time planning in preparation.
